# EscapeRoom
A game that simulates a user trying to escape a multi-room house with a surprise lying behind every door. 

# How to play
1. You must find a way to exit the house.
2. The house has 5 areas Bedroom, Bathroom, Living Room, Kitchen, Basement
3. Each Area Has two doors Each leading to another area.
4. One Room Will have a door leading outside. The door is assigned randomly at the beginning of the game.
5. The Bathroom will not lead outside.
6. You have 7 doors to open before you get caught.
7. You will have the option to collect a special item that is present in each room by first correctly answering a question.
8. Opening a door or incorrectly answering a question will cost you one life.
9. The game ends if you manage to escape or once your lives hit zero.
